# Welcome to marginfi

marginfi is a fully-decentralized borrowing and lending protocol built on Solana. With marginfi, you can access native yield, embedded risk systems, and off-chain data plug-ins all in one place. Use marginfi to access the margin you need, when you need it.

## Features {{ anchor: false }}

<b>End-to-End Risk Engine</b>: Constantly monitors the health of each individual bank, covering the entire protocol's risk comprehensively. {{ className: 'lead' }}

<b>In-House Liquidators</b>: Relies solely on in-house liquidators rather than external ones, ensuring a robust liquidation process. {{ className: 'lead' }}

<b>Market Depth and Recovery Modeling</b>: Gathers data to model market depth and recovery time, enabling predictions of future liquidity in various market scenarios. {{ className: 'lead' }}

<b>Dynamic Risk System</b>: Plans to implement live bank updates directly from risk models for a dynamic and real-time risk management system. {{ className: 'lead' }}

<b>Global Borrowing and Lending</b>: The protocol enables borrowing and lending
of margin under a unified global context, streamlining the process for traders. {{
  className: 'lead',
}}

<b>Leverage Trading</b>: marginfi supports trading with leverage, empowering
traders to maximize their market participation. {{ className: 'lead' }}

<b>Comprehensive Market Access</b>: Traders can optimize their financial
exposure across over 100 DeFi projects on Solana, including spot, futures, and
options markets. {{ className: 'lead' }}
